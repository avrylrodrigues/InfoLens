About InfoLens

InfoLens is a platform designed to promote informed and responsible digital news consumption by helping users understand how online information is presented.

Why InfoLens exists

The rapid spread of online news and social media content has made it increasingly difficult for users to assess credibility. Misleading headlines and emotionally charged language often influence opinion and trust.

That is where InfoLens comes in!

How InfoLens works

InfoLens allows users to submit links to news articles for analysis.

The platform examines the tone of the language, emotional wording, and structural features commonly associated with misleading or credible content.

Ethical approach

InfoLens does not determine truth or falsehood.

Instead, it promotes critical thinking by providing transparent explanations that allows users to form their own informed judgments.

Intended users

InfoLens is designed for students, researchers, and everyday readers who want to improve media literacy and engage more critically with digital news.
