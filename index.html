About uncloud

uncloud is a platform designed to promote informed and responsible digital news consumption by helping users understand how online information is presented.

Why uncloud exists

The rapid spread of online news and social media content has made it increasingly difficult for users to assess credibility. Misleading headlines and emotionally charged language often influence opinion and trust.

That is where uncloud comes in!

How uncloud works

uncloud allows users to submit links to news articles for analysis.

The platform examines the tone of the language, emotional wording, and structural features commonly associated with misleading or credible content.

Ethical approach

uncloud does not determine truth or falsehood.

Instead, it promotes critical thinking by providing transparent explanations that allows users to form their own informed judgments.

Intended users

uncloud is designed for students, researchers, and everyday readers who want to improve media literacy and engage more critically with digital news.
